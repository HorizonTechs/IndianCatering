About Catering

Tradition since 1889

The Catering was established in Howrah, West Bengal. By using top Indian ingredients, we provided variety of foods and rich taste, since it was founded.

We have served number of customers and we received good feedback everytime. Customer's statisfaction is our main motive thus we take recommendations if any changes are needed other than our standard receipe before preparation.

We serve both veg and non-veg dishes, we take special care of vegitarian ingredients so that it doesn't get in contact with non-vegitarian dishes.

Our Top Menu Items


Murg Makhani: Indian Butter Chicken

It gets its lovely, distinctive flavors from the tangy tomatoes, yogurt, and smoky kasuri methi


Rogan Josh (Red Lamb)

Whole spices along with ground and fresh spices are what add layer upon layer of flavor to this Indian curry


Malai Kofta

Malai (meaning creamy in Hindi) kofta is the perfect vegetarian alternative to meatballs.


Naan (Leavened Indian Flatbread)

There is no Indian bread that is more famous than naan. They are perfect for mopping up the gravy in Indian curries or other dishes.


Ras Malai (Milk Juice Breads)

It is a desert soaked in malai (clotted cream) flavoured with cardamom and saffron.
